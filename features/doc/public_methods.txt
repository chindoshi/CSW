This file contains lists, for our reference, of public methods available within any given
cucumber matcher, as well as for each page object.  The intent is to help us discover the
navigation, assertion, and other useful methods available to our cucumber tests.

These lists were produced by the '/^the public (.*) methods are logged$/' matcher in debug_steps.rb,
but the non-English methods were removed.  This list will grow as we add mix more modules and methods
into the World.
